Services

Top-tier finishes start with proper prep. Explore our offerings and recommended use-cases below.

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, trim, doors. Primer selection based on substrate and sheen change. Low-odor, fast-dry options available.

Exterior Painting

Weather-tolerant systems for wood, masonry, fiber cement, and more. Full surface prep including scraping, sanding, priming, and caulking.

Staining & Wood Finishes

Decks, doors, beams, and trim. Transparent to solid-color stains; clear coats for protection.

Cabinetry Refinishing

Degrease, sand, prime (bonding/shellac where appropriate), then enamel topcoats for a factory-like finish.

Concrete & Masonry Coatings (Epoxy & Urethane)

Moisture testing, mechanical profile (if needed), epoxy primers, and abrasion-resistant urethane topcoats.

Drywall Repair & Surface Prep

Patching, skim coats, sanding, and problem-primers (e.g., stain/blocking, adhesion) to set up a flawless finish.

Waterproofing / Weatherproofing

Sealants, elastomeric systems, and masonry waterproofers to keep the elements out.

Rust-Blocking Metal Coatings

Surface prep, rust converters, and corrosion-inhibitive primers before durable metal topcoats.

Window Sealing / Caulking

Clean removal, proper backer-rod (as needed), and pro-grade sealants for longevity and efficiency.
